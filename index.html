A storage network owned by the people who use it

Fula is a decentralized storage network governed by Fula Governance Association — a DAO in which every FULA token holder has the opportunity to participate in network decisions.

How governance works Explore the FULA token

What Fula Governance Association does

Governs the network

Protocol decisions, network parameters and the direction of the Fula network are decided through the association, with FULA token holders able to take part.

Stewards the FULA token

Token issuance, distribution and treasury management sit with Fula Governance Association — not with any single contributing company.

Awards grants

Teams that add value to the network can receive grants. Grantees build, maintain and market the network while remaining independent entities.

Issues FEC NFTs

Founding Engineer Club NFTs and related collections are issued and managed by the association.

Building something that adds value to Fula?

Grants are available for work that strengthens the network. If you have something that adds value, get in touch with Fula Governance members.
