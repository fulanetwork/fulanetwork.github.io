Privacy Policy

Effective date: 20 July 2026

Important Disclaimer

This website (fula.network) is owned and operated by Fula Governance Association ("FGA"), an association organized under the laws of Switzerland. This policy covers only this Website. It does not cover websites, applications or hardware operated by grantees or other independent parties, including Functionland Inc, which maintain their own policies.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how information is collected, used and disclosed when you visit fula.network (the "Website"). FGA aims to collect as little personal information as possible.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit the Website, limited information may be collected automatically through analytics tooling, including IP address (anonymized where possible), browser type and version, operating system, pages visited and time spent, and referring website address. This is used solely to understand Website traffic and improve the experience. No personally identifiable information is intentionally collected through these tools.

2.2 Information You Provide

If you contact FGA by email or another channel linked from this Website — for example to ask about a grant — any personal information you provide, such as your name, email address or project details, is used only to respond to and assess your inquiry.

2.3 Information We Do Not Collect

FGA does not collect through this Website:

  • Private keys, seed phrases or wallet credentials — never share these with anyone
  • Know Your Customer (KYC) or identity verification data
  • Payment card or banking information
  • Personal information from children under 18

3. Blockchain Data

The Fula network and the FULA token operate on public blockchains. Wallet addresses, balances, staking activity and transactions recorded on a public blockchain are inherently public, are not controlled by FGA, and cannot be altered or deleted by FGA or by you. If you connect a wallet to a page on this Website, the address you connect may be visible to that page in order to display your own on-chain information. Treat all on-chain activity as permanent and public.

4. Use of Information

Information collected through the Website is used solely to analyze traffic and usage patterns, improve Website content and user experience, respond to inquiries, and administer the grant programme.

5. Cookies and Tracking

The Website uses cookies and similar technologies for analytics purposes. You may disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect your experience. By continuing to use the Website, you consent to the use of cookies as described here.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

FGA does not sell, trade or otherwise transfer your information to third parties, except to analytics service providers for the purposes described above, and where required by applicable law, regulation or legal process.

7. Third-Party Services

The Website may link to third-party websites, platforms and services, including decentralized applications, exchanges, block explorers and grantee websites such as fx.land. FGA has no control over and assumes no responsibility for their content, privacy policies or practices. Your use of them is at your own risk and subject to their own terms.

8. Data Retention

Analytics data is retained in accordance with the default retention policies of the analytics providers used. FGA does not maintain independent databases of personal information about Website visitors. Grant correspondence is retained only as long as needed to assess and administer the grant.

9. Data Security

FGA implements reasonable technical measures to protect the Website. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is completely secure, and FGA does not guarantee absolute security.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct or delete personal data held about you. For requests concerning this Website, contact FGA. Note that information recorded on a public blockchain cannot be modified or deleted by FGA.

11. International Users

The Website is operated from Switzerland. If you access it from elsewhere, you do so at your own initiative and are responsible for compliance with your local laws.

12. Limitation of Liability

FGA assumes no liability for any data processing conducted by third parties or grantees, any loss of tokens, digital assets or funds, the privacy practices of third-party platforms, exchanges or decentralized applications linked from the Website, or any decisions made by users based on information presented on the Website.

13. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Switzerland, and any disputes shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Switzerland.

14. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

15. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries concerning this Website, contact Fula Governance Association. For matters relating to Functionland Inc, its hardware, applications or website, contact Functionland Inc directly at fx.land.
